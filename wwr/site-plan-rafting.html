Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to instill a sense of fun, adventure, and excitement in our customers, and to market what we offer to a larger group of people.

Audience

Who are the target customers? Describe their life (or business) situation (age, financial situation, interests, and so on): The target customers would be family or friend groups on vacation. They would likely be teens, young adults, and families who are interesting in building friendships, having fun, and being in the outdoors. What do they want? They want a fun, adventurous experience that is found in rafting, and being able to enjoy vacations with friends or family in the outdoors. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? They expect to find snippits of the rafting adventures (e.g. pictures or short video clips), pricing, find different water routes or rafts to fit their needs.
